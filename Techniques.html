Breathing Techniques

Box Breathing

Box breathing is a simple technique that involves taking slow, deep breaths while visualizing the four sides of a box. It can help calm your mind and reduce stress.

Alternate Nostril Breathing

Alternate nostril breathing is a technique that involves breathing through one nostril at a time. It can help balance your energy and promote a sense of calm and focus.

Diaphragmatic Breathing

Diaphragmatic breathing, also known as belly breathing, involves deep inhalations that expand your diaphragm. It can help reduce anxiety, improve focus, and increase oxygen levels.
